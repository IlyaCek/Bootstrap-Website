The Cottage

It is a three bedroom cottage featuring modern living and original features such as beamed ceilings, sleeping up to 6 people.

The accommodation comprises two double and one twin bedroom, two bathrooms, living area, dining area and kitchen.

To the rear of the house there is a raised area comprising a patio with BBQ and firepit set in a wildflower meadow.

The house is bright, airy, and snug having recently been insulated to the highest standards.

There is oil fired central heating, a wood burning stove, satellite television and WIFI.

Outside is a small shed for storing bikes etc and the washing machine and freezer are also located here.

Facilities

  • Double bedroom on ground floor
  • One double and one twin bedroom upstairs
  • Fully furnished kitchen
  • Bathrooms upstairs and on ground floor
  • Bedding provided
  • Dining area with table and chairs
  • Living room with ultra comfy L shaped sofa, satellite tv and wood burning stove
  • Entrance hall with hanging space for coats
  • Shed for storing bikes and containing washing machine and freezer
